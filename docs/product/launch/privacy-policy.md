# Versefold Privacy Policy (DRAFT — requires legal review before publication)

_Last updated: [DATE]_

## The short version

Versefold is a quiet Bible app. Reading requires no account. Your highlights, notes, and
bookmarks stay on your device. We don't show ads, we don't sell data, and we don't build
advertising profiles.

## What we collect

**Nothing, for reading.** The Bible text (KJV) is bundled with the app. Reading, highlighting,
noting, and bookmarking happen entirely on your device.

**An anonymous device identifier**, when you use the study layer (Unfold, Ask, guided studies).
It is a random ID, not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID. We use it to apply fair-use
limits and to let you delete your AI history.

**The passages and questions you send to the study layer.** When you ask for an explanation or
a study, the selected passage reference and your question are sent to our server and processed
by a third-party AI provider (OpenAI) under a contract that prohibits training on your data.
Your private notes are never included unless you explicitly choose to include them for a
specific request.

**Feedback you send us** during the beta, if you choose to send it.

**Account data (optional, future).** If you enable sync, we use Apple's Sign in with Apple and
iCloud (CloudKit); your library syncs through your own iCloud account, not our servers.

## What we never do

- No ads. No data sales. No third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.
- No reading-behavior profiles. We do not track which passages you read.
- No using your private notes for AI without explicit per-use consent.
- No training AI models on your content.

## Deletion and export

Settings → Privacy lets you export all of your data as a file, delete your AI history (from
your device and our server), or delete everything. Deleting the app removes all local data.

## Data retention

AI artifacts you chose to save are kept until you delete them. Feedback is kept for the beta
period. Server logs rotate within 30 days.

## Children

Versefold is not directed at children under 13.
