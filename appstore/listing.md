## Description (4000 chars max)

Versefold is a quiet place to read, study, and remember Scripture. It opens
straight to the Word — no feed, no streaks, no social layer, nothing pulling
at your attention. Scripture first. Everything else quiet.

READ
Open to your last reading location and simply read. The full KJV lives on
your device and works completely offline. Switch to NIV or the Amplified
Bible and flip between translations instantly — compare a verse without
losing your place. Clean typography, warm ivory pages, and themes that stay
out of the way.

MARK IT LIKE A PAPER BIBLE
Press and hold, then drag your finger across the words that matter. Choose a
hand-drawn marker stroke or a penned underline — your ink lands on exactly
the words you chose, wraps naturally with the text, and can be erased or
layered just like the margins of a well-loved Bible. Notes leave a small
hand-penned mark beside the verse in every translation.

UNDERSTAND
Select any passage and Unfold it: a careful, deeper explanation grounded in
the text itself. Every explanation is clearly labeled as AI-assisted
commentary — never styled like Scripture — and carries a Basis section citing
the passages it draws on. Ask your own question about any passage and get an
answer rooted in the Word, not the internet.

STUDY
Build a personal guided study from any verse, book, or theme — choose the
length, the minutes per day, and the depth. Each day brings readings,
context, reflection questions, and a prayer prompt. Pause anytime. Nothing
expires, and there are no streaks to lose.

REMEMBER
Turn a verse into a confession card: the Scripture in full, with a short
confession in your own voice built on its meaning — crafted with help if you
want it, always editable. Save it to Photos or share it. "Meditate" in
Scripture is the Hebrew hagah — to mutter, to keep the Word on your lips.
Versefold is built around that practice.

SEARCH BY MEANING
Search for "confidence and strength in the Lord" and find the passages that
carry that meaning — not just the ones that contain those words.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No account. No sign-up. No ads. No analytics trackers. Your highlights,
notes, and studies stay on your device — export them or delete them anytime.

The Word before the phone.

## Review notes (for the App Review team)

Versefold requires no account and no login. AI features (Unfold, studies,
confession suggestions) call our server at api.versefold.app; they are
clearly labeled AI-assisted commentary, cite their scriptural basis, and are
never presented as Scripture. The KJV text is public domain; NIV and AMP are
served under license via API.Bible (American Bible Society). No payments, no
subscriptions in this version.
